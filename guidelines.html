COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

Whistla works best when we all work together. Follow these guidelines to keep our community helpful and trustworthy.

✅ DO

  • Report issues you actually see in person
  • Be specific about location (lot numbers, levels, landmarks)
  • Include license plate numbers when safe to do so
  • Mark reports as resolved when the issue is fixed
  • Share alerts to help find vehicle owners
  • Be kind and helpful in your descriptions

❌ DON'T

  • Submit false or prank reports
  • Use offensive language in reports
  • Report vehicles just because you don't like them
  • Share personal information beyond license plates
  • Spam the feed with repeated reports
  • Use the app for harassment

Consequences of Violations
Users who violate these guidelines may have their reports removed and may be banned from the platform.

Reporting Violations
See a false or inappropriate report? Use the "Report as False" feature or just whistle
