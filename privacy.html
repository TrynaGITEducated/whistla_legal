PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect
Whistler collects minimal information to provide its services:

  • Location Data: Used only to show nearby alerts and tag report locations. Never stored with personal identifiers.
  • Report Content: The text, tags, and optional license plate information you submit in reports.
  • Device Information: Basic device type and app version for analytics.

2. How We Use Information
- To display relevant alerts to community members
- To improve app performance and features
- To prevent spam and fraudulent reports

3. Data Sharing
We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Reports are visible to all app users as part of the community feed.

4. Data Retention
Reports remain in the system until marked resolved or deleted. You can clear locally cached data through the Settings screen.

5. Your Rights
You may request deletion of your reports by contacting us. You can also clear local data at any time.

6. Children's Privacy
Whistler is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact Us
Questions? Contact privacy@whistler.app
