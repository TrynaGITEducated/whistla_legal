HELP & FAQ

Last Updated: June 14, 2026
How do I submit a report?
Tap the "REPORT" tab, select relevant tags, describe the issue, add location and vehicle details, then tap "WHISTLE →".
Who sees my reports?
All Whistla users can see reports in the feed. Reports are anonymous — your identity is not shared.
How do I mark an alert as resolved?
Tap on any report to open details, then tap "MARK RESOLVED". This helps others know the issue is fixed.
Can I share alerts outside the app?
Yes! Tap "SHARE ALERT" to share via WhatsApp, SMS, email, or social media.
What if someone submits a false report?
Use the "Report as False" feature. Repeated false reports will result in a ban.
Does the app work offline?
Yes! Reports are saved locally and sync when you reconnect to the internet.
How do I clear my data?
Go to Settings → Privacy & Data → Clear Local Data.
Is Whistla free?
Yes, the core features are free. Premium features may be added in the future.
